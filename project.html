How To Play

This is a two player game - Player 1 goes first - when you click 'start' you have 13 seconds to clear all the rocks by clicking them in sequential order (lowest to highest).

You play 8 rounds in total - so you could draw 4-4, or annihilate your opponent and win 8-0. Press Start during your game for an extra challenge...

P.S. The speed increases after every player turn.
